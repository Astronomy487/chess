Chess Sandbox

Chess Sandbox is a generalized sandbox for creating and playing chess variants and chess-adjacent games. You can use it to play from a custom board position, or you can define new pieces and play with them. It is accomplished with client-side JavaScript.

FENGEN

A supplemental user-friendly tool for creating positions using the six standard pieces on two teams.
